ShikkhaJar is a mobile-first, bilingual app tailored for Bangladeshi parents, tutors, and students to track private tuition sessions, sync attendance calendars, manage payments, and enhance tutor-parent collaboration.

Features:

One-Tap Attendance Tracking
Mark teaching days in a beautiful, colour-coded calendar.

Auto-Renewing Tuition Cycles
Define 12-day (or custom) contracts. Get payment reminders and reset cycles automatically.


Collaboration Mode
Invite your tutor/parent to share and sync calendar updates offline/online.

Exam & Portfolio Management
Tutors can upload results and export performance history for future clients.

Smart Notifications
Payment reminders from Day 10. Rescheduling alerts. Post-class updates.

Clean, Animated UI
Mobile-first, intuitive design in Bangla & English. Built to work without tutorials.

Dark/Light Mode + Language Toggle
Customizable themes and language preferences.

Why ShikkhaJar?

Most Bangladeshi families use pen and paper or note-taking apps to count how many times a tutor came. ShikkhaJar automates and visualises this process, making it collaborative. It's not just tracking, it's trust-building between students, parents, and tutors.
